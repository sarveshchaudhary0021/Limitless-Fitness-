Nutrition & Health

Fuel Your Body

Expert dietary plans and critical medical guidelines for safe training.

Vegan Plan

Focus: Plant-based proteins (lentils, tofu, chickpeas).

  • High fiber for digestion
  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Avoid highly processed faux meats

Veg / Non-Veg Plan

Focus: Balanced macro intake (chicken, eggs, paneer).

  • High quality amino acids
  • Dairy & eggs for dense nutrition
  • Avoid excess red meat and fried items

Medical: Hypertension (BP)

Training: Focus on steady-state cardio. Avoid heavy isometric holds, 1RM lifting, or exercises where your head is below your heart (e.g., decline bench, heavy deadlifts).

Diet: Follow DASH diet principles. Strict sodium restriction. Increase potassium-rich foods like bananas and spinach.

Medical: Heart Conditions

Training: Extremely light to moderate aerobic activity. ALWAYS consult a cardiologist before starting. Avoid high-intensity interval training (HIIT). Stop if you feel dizzy or have chest pain.

Diet: Mediterranean diet focus. Eat Omega-3 rich foods. Strictly avoid trans fats, excess saturated fats, and high-sugar drinks.

Gym Etiquette

What to Avoid

Keep yourself and others safe. Discipline is the core of Limitless.

Ego Lifting

Lifting heavier than you can handle with strict form leads to severe injuries. Focus on muscle contraction, not momentum.

Phone Camping

Don't sit on machines during peak hours scrolling through social media. Rest efficiently and let others work in.

Leaving Weights

Re-rack your weights after use. Leaving plates on the bar or dumbbells on the floor is dangerous and disrespectful.
