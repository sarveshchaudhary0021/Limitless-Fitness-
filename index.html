Nutrition & Health

Fuel Your Body

Expert dietary plans and critical medical guidelines for safe training.

Vegan

Vegan Plan

Plant-based proteins (lentils, tofu, chickpeas)
  • High fibre for optimal digestion
  • Rich in antioxidants and micronutrients
  • Avoid highly processed faux meats
Balanced

Veg / Non-Veg Plan

Balanced macro intake (chicken, eggs, paneer)
  • High-quality amino acid profile
  • Dairy & eggs for dense nutrition
  • Limit red meat and fried items
Medical

Hypertension (BP)

Training: Focus on steady-state cardio. Avoid heavy isometric holds, 1RM lifting, or exercises where your head is below your heart. Diet: Follow DASH diet principles. Strict sodium restriction. Increase potassium-rich foods like bananas and spinach.
⚠ Consult your doctor before starting any programme.
Medical

Heart Conditions

Training: Light to moderate aerobic activity only. Stop if dizzy or in pain. Avoid HIIT. Diet: Mediterranean diet focus. Omega-3 rich foods. Avoid trans fats and high-sugar drinks.
⚠ ALWAYS consult a cardiologist before starting.
Gym Etiquette

What to Avoid

Keep yourself and others safe. Discipline is the core of Limitless.

✕

Ego Lifting

Lifting heavier than you can handle with strict form leads to severe injuries. Focus on muscle contraction, not momentum.

✕

Phone Camping

Don't sit on machines during peak hours scrolling social media. Rest efficiently and let others work in.

✕

Leaving Weights

Re-rack your weights after use. Leaving plates on the floor is dangerous and disrespectful to fellow members.
